Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology

Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology (JIET), Jodhpur is an esteemed and prestigious institution of Engineering and Management in Rajasthan, devoted to the cause of promoting technical and scientific literacy and temperament in the young inquisitive minds right from its inception in 2003. The multi-disciplinary campus JIET is a trustworthy name in Rajasthan as it provides multiple opportunities for gaining insights into professional education through the availability of resources.


The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated with Bikaner Technical University, Bikaner. It is the only engineering institution in Rajasthan (among all government and private colleges) accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ Grade, NBA Accreditation in four UG programs since 2011 (three times in a row) and Autonomous Status granted by UGC. JIET has been ranked first in the state by the affiliating university continuously three times in a row (Session 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21) and ranked as per the QIV ranking of BTU. The institute also has UGC recognition under Section 2(f). The institute runs six UG programs, five PG programs. JIET is also the Approved Research Centre (Ph.D.) of the University in four disciplines.


JIET Jodhpur Admission Dates: JIET has started accepting application forms for all the programs. The last date to apply for the same is August 14, 2022. The candidates can get the application from the official website of the institute by paying the application fee of INR 500.
